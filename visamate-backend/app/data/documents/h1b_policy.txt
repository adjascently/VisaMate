H-1B is a temporary professional work visa requiring a bachelor's degree or higher.

Key Rules:
• Sponsoring employer must file Form I-129
• Prevailing wage requirements apply
• Position must require specialty knowledge
• Lottery occurs each March

Cap-Gap:
F-1 students with pending/approved H-1B may receive automatic extension of OPT until September 30.

Travel:
Travel during H-1B processing is restricted unless student has valid F-1 status or valid H-1B visa.

Employer Changes:
H-1B workers may change employers with an H-1B transfer filing.
