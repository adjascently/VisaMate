STEM OPT is a 24-month extension of post-completion OPT for students with qualifying STEM degrees.
Total possible OPT = 12 months + 24 months = 36 months.

Eligibility Requirements:
• Job must be directly related to STEM degree
• Employer must be E-Verify enrolled
• Completed Form I-983 Training Plan must be approved by DSO
• Must file STEM OPT before post-completion OPT ends

Unemployment Rules:
Students on STEM OPT receive **an additional 60 days** of unemployment.
Total allowed unemployment = **150 days** (90 + 60).

Employment Requirements:
• Must work at least 20 hrs/week
• Cannot be self-employed
• Cannot work for a staffing agency or 1099 contractor
• Must update Form I-983 if employer changes

Travel:
Allowed if student has:
• STEM OPT receipt notice OR EAD (preferred)
• Valid F-1 visa
• Valid passport
• Signed I-20
• Employment letter
