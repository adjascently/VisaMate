CPT (Curricular Practical Training) permits employment when it is integral to a student’s academic program.

CPT Requirements:
• Must have completed one full academic year
• CPT must be tied to curriculum or course requirement
• Job MUST be related to major
• Must have a job offer before CPT I-20 is issued
• Work authorization is printed directly on I-20 (no USCIS filing)

Types:
• Part-time CPT: up to 20 hrs/week
• Full-time CPT: more than 20 hrs/week

CPT and OPT Interaction:
Full-time CPT for 12 months or more = student loses eligibility for OPT.

Travel:
Allowed with CPT I-20, valid visa, valid passport.
