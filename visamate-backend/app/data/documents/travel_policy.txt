F-1 Travel Rules:

To re-enter the U.S. an F-1 student must have:
• A valid passport
• A valid F-1 visa
• A signed I-20 (within 12 months or 6 months if on OPT)
• Proof of enrollment or employment

Travel on OPT requires:
• EAD card
• Proof of employment
• Signed I-20 within last 6 months

Grace Periods:
• 60-day grace period after OPT ends
• No employment allowed during grace period
