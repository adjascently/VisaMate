Unemployment Rules for F-1 OPT and STEM OPT:

Post-completion OPT:
• Maximum 90 days unemployment
• Begins on the EAD start date
• Any day without qualifying employment counts
• Volunteer work counts if related to degree and 20+ hrs/week

STEM OPT:
• Additional 60 days unemployment
• Total: 150 days across all OPT

Exceeding Limits:
• Student loses F-1 status
• Must depart U.S., transfer to new program, or change status
• DSO may terminate SEVIS record
